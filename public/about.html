Restaurant Born
from Vision

Olive & Fire was created from a vision to bring elegance, warmth, and meaningful together under one roof. Founded with a passion for refined dining experiences, it reflects a journey inspired by tradition, dedication, and a deep respect for detail. From the beginning, the idea was simple: a restaurant should feel welcoming, comfortable, and effortlessly sophisticated. Every element of Olive & Fire is designed to make guests feel valued—whether they are visiting for a celebration or a quiet evening away from routine. Today, Olive & Fire continues to grow as a destination known for its atmosphere and service. Yet its true success is measured in something far simpler: the feeling guests carry with them when they leave.
